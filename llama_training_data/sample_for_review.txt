--- Entry 1 (original label: 1) ---
CLAUSE:
The software shall be installed and made operational at Egyptian Space Agency's technical facility. Delivery shall be confirmed through an official handover report. Egyptian Space Agency shall prepare the technical environment per InnoSoft Egypt's specifications. Upon formal notification of completion by InnoSoft Egypt, Egyptian Space Agency shall have the right to withhold acceptance for up to eighteen (18) months without this constituting an unjustified refusal, and InnoSoft Egypt shall have no right to claim deemed delivery during that period.

RESPONSE:
VERDICT: INVALID

VIOLATION TYPE: unlawful_refusal_to_take_delivery

LEGAL ANALYSIS: Article 655 — the employer must promptly take delivery and unjustified refusal after formal notification results in deemed delivery; an eighteen-month withholding period without any deemed-delivery consequence is irreconcilable with Article 655.

ENHANCEMENT: Revise this clause to comply with Article 655. The current provision violates the statutory requirement. Consult the relevant article to restructure the clause appropriately.
